Swivel can issue licenses automatically the moment a customer completes a purchase. When you configure a webhook in your store, it sends a signed HTTP request to a Swivel endpoint every time an order is paid. Swivel verifies the signature, maps the purchased products to your configured SKUs, and creates the corresponding licenses — no manual steps required.

## How it works

1. A customer completes checkout in your Shopify or WooCommerce store.
2. The store sends a signed webhook event to your Swivel endpoint.
3. Swivel verifies the HMAC signature using your webhook secret.
4. Swivel maps each line item's SKU to a product in your account.
5. A license is created and the customer receives their key.

## Idempotency

Swivel deduplicates webhook events by the unique delivery ID sent in each request. If the same event is delivered more than once — due to a network retry or a re-delivery from your store — Swivel returns a `200 OK` without creating a duplicate license. You do not need to guard against this on your end.
