## How Swivel Licensing works

When you purchase a Swivel Audio plugin, a license is automatically created and linked to your email address. You then activate that license inside the plugin on each computer you use, up to the allowed machine limit.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Purchase a plugin">
    Complete your purchase on the Swivel Audio store. Your license is created automatically and a receipt is sent to your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your license key">
    Visit your [account licenses page](/licenses/find-your-license) or check your purchase receipt email to retrieve your license key.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate in the plugin">
    Open the plugin in your DAW, enter your license key in the activation panel, and click **Activate**. The plugin connects to the Swivel server and unlocks processing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage machines">
    Deactivate a machine you no longer use from your [account page](/licenses/deactivation) to free a slot for a new computer.
  </Step>
</Steps>
